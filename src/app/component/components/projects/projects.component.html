Integrated Excise Supply Chain Management System (IESCMS)

Developed IESCMS Solution to ensure product authenticity and accountability across all Uttar Pradesh Excise Supply Chain transactions.

  • Managed application workflows including product requests, approvals, and transport pass generation
  • IESCMS Solution will be used by the consumer to verify the authenticity of the product sold.
  • Providing an inherent use/benefit for the manufacturers, government and its citizens for using this solution.
  • The solution should be accountable for all the transactions happening in multiple departments related to the Uttar Pradesh Excise in Supply Chain Management.
